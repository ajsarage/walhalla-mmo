====================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
====================================

Q: My party invite expired!
A: Invites last 60 seconds. Re-send with /party invite <player>

Q: I'm not getting party XP!
A: Stay within 30 blocks of party members. Check /party list.

Q: I picked the wrong talent!
A: Type /skills, spend 10,000 WCoin to reset all points.

Q: My daily streak reset!
A: You missed 2 consecutive days (48+ hours). Start fresh!

Q: Party teleport isn't working!
A: Costs 100 WCoin. Check your balance with /walhalla or /menu.

Q: Can't see action bar messages!
A: They update every 0.5s automatically. Check your client settings.
